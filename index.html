I'm a designer with an aptitude for analytical thinking and an appetite for play. Currently shaping tech for social impact at UNICEF Innovation, where I facilitate design guidance for solutions we build and invest in. I think of my practice as a way to bridge intention and outcome.

Previously, I was a designer at Salesforce in San Francisco — see some of that work in this overview. With a background in graphic design, I've also crafted brand identities and ads in the past. Find other curiosities and half-baked ideas on my corner of the internet.
