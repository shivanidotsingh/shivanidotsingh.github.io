Cultural Edutainment for the South Asian Diaspora

This project aims to bridge the cultural gap faced by the South Asian diaspora in the US, particularly focusing on the younger generation (Gen Z).

1. Discovery

The challenge was to create engaging educational content that resonates with this demographic, fostering a deeper connection to their heritage while remaining relevant to their contemporary lives.

2. Development

We addressed this by developing interactive and accessible educational modules leveraging digital platforms and storytelling techniques.

3. Delivery

The solution encompasses a series of digital experiences, including animated shorts, interactive quizzes, and short-form documentaries, all designed to be shared on platforms like TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

4. What we learned

Through this process, we recognized the immense potential of digital-first content to create meaningful cultural connections for a generation that heavily relies on these platforms for information and entertainment.
